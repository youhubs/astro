Support Astro Robotics

Your donation helps us continue our work in innovative robotics education.

Do you have a Kroger Plus card?

Link your Kroger's Plus Shopper's Card to Astro Robotics Kroger Community Rewards account. For every purchase our supporters make at Kroger, Kroger will donate some percentages to Astro Robotics. Your spending information is 100% confidential and Kroger does not share this information with any organization which it funds.

Register online at kroger.com.

  • Login to your Kroger account using your email address/password.
  • Under "My Account", click on "Community Rewards".
  • Search for "Astro Robotics" either by name or MW141, then click "Enroll".
  • You will see "Astro Robotics" on your Donation Summary.

*Enrolling in this program DOES NOT affect your fuel points earnings.

*Kroger requires that you re-register your card with your chosen organization every year.

Your support is very important for our growth and is greatly appreciated. More details can be found here krogercommunityrewards.com.
